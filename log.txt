### v1.0.3 (2026-06-22)

- Restyled skill GUI to TFC style: centered light-gray panel, dark text, gold progress bars, clearer title

- Removed duplicate skill damage scaling: deleted the extra LivingIncomingDamageEvent multiplier, keeping only the attribute modifier
- Perfect + Master attack scaling corrected from ~4.34x to the intended ~2.55x
- Mining speed bonus logic preserved unchanged
- Forge XP changed from flat 1 to random 1~3 per craft
